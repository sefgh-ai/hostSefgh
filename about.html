How SEFGH Works

The Simple Version

1

It Listens Carefully

SEFGH meticulously reads your full description, whether it's a few lines or a detailed essay, precisely identifying your true requirements and intent.

2

It Explores Widely

Utilizing creative angles and diverse linguistic interpretations, SEFGH scours GitHub for projects that align with your request, even if phrased differently or in other languages.

3

It Evaluates Thoughtfully

Every found project is rigorously compared against your criteria, receiving a clear similarity score. Only the top-matched options are presented, pre-ranked and explained.
